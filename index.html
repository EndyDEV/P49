Some Interesting facts about Camels

  • Camels store energy and water in their humps
  • Camels have padded feet which help them walk on sand easily

Some Interesting facts about Chameleons

  • Chameleons have long and sticky tongues which help them catch prey
  • Chameleons can camoflouge themselves to hide from predators
  • Chameleons can look in two different directions at the same time
